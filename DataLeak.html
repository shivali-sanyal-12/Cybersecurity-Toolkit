Data Leak Detection & Prevention

Overview

Data leaks happen when sensitive information is exposed unintentionally. Detecting leaks early and preventing them is vital for protecting privacy and maintaining security compliance.

  • Common sensitive data: Emails, passwords, credit card numbers, Social Security numbers.
  • Leak sources: Misconfigured databases, insider threats, phishing attacks, poor access controls.
  • Detection techniques: Pattern matching, keyword scanning, anomaly detection.
  • Prevention: Encryption, data masking, access controls, employee training.
