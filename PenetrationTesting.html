Penetration Testing (Pentesting) is a simulated cyberattack against your computer system to check for exploitable vulnerabilities.

  • Reconnaissance: Gathering info about target system.
  • Scanning: Identifying live hosts, open ports, and services.
  • Exploitation: Attempting to exploit vulnerabilities.
  • Post-Exploitation: Assessing the impact and maintaining access.
  • Reporting: Documenting findings & remediation steps.

  • Burp Suite: Web vulnerability scanner.
  • Wireshark: Network traffic analyzer.
  • John the Ripper: Password cracking tool.
