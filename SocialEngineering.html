Social Engineering is a psychological manipulation technique hackers use to trick people into revealing confidential information or performing actions that compromise security.

  • Phishing: Fraudulent emails or messages to steal credentials.
  • Pretexting: Creating fabricated stories to gain trust.
  • Baiting: Offering something enticing to get victims to act.
  • Tailgating: Gaining physical access by following authorized personnel.
